Making the Magic

Spencer works in a basement in the light of a single window and the bong that is always cherrying in the corner. Sometimes when he hits a roadblock in his painting he sits down for a good session of shoot the nazis or skyrim. He doesn't like to cut his oil paints which means that they don't last as long but it produces more vibrant colors and lets him play with things like texture

There's also some other cool things that Spencer does that makes his art cool and unique and things and stuff. In general Spencer uses paintbrushes to put paint on canvases.

Finding Inspiration

Spencer really likes animals. Like a lot. Like a lot a lot. He has many fond memories of watching animal planet and channels this into his artwork. Spencer holds his nature documentaries to a high standard. For instance he was very upset about the honey badger documentary he watched last week because it played to existing honey badger stereotypes instead of providing the hard hitting badger facts he craves.

Sometimes Spencer paints things that aren't animals. Like that one time Spencer painted a bike wheel. It was a pretty bike wheel. The main thing is that the composition and the colors are engaging though. And Badger Facts. He's all about those Badger facts.
